Membership
What We Can Do for Your Business
We connect businesses to information, partners and opportunity to catalyze growth . We amplify our members and open doors to vast networks of business and thought leaders, government and other stakeholders to ensure that our members drive industry innovation and are at the heart of policy discussions affecting business.
Representation for Policy Advocacy
High-level representation through our direct access and relationships with local and U.S. government agencies charged with policy development and enhancement to support business and economic growth.
Market Access and Commercial Interaction
Unmatched U.S. market access through our relationship with the U.S. Embassy and the U.S. Chamber of Commerce.
Business Networking Opportunities
Local and cross-border business networking made possible through our signature networking events and forums throughout the calendar year.
Business Matchmaking Facilitation
We help you identify and engage with potential business partners within our membership across various sectors to meet your business needs.
Insight on Topical Issues
Timely access to insight on issues relevant to member business and commercial interests delivered through our speaker series events and member bulletins
Enhanced visibility
Our various platforms and member engagement forums give you unique opportunities to position your brand, increase visibility and gain valuable brand validation.

Platinum Membership
Annual Subscription - $2,000
Every business is unique, and so we tailor our partnerships to deliver value and support that aligns with your business priorities.

From being a part of high level round table discussions with key leaders in both business and government circles, to contributing your voice on strategic issues that your business requires to thrive over the long term, the AmCham Platinum partnership supports you in building a solid business ecosystem.

PT
Patron Circle
Annual Subscription - $9,000
Patrons are businesses looking to go above and beyond by making an impact on the Chamber, working to shape policy and gain recognition as leaders.

Patrons are of great value to us, since their contribution and support help us to catalyze impact together, thereby influencing the Chamber’s future.

Patrons are entitled to several exclusive privileges, special recognition, extra visibility and incentives.

Due to its exclusive nature, the Patron level is by invitation only and limited to a few companies annually.

American Chamber of Commerce Directory

The U.S. Chamber of Commerce is proud to provide an extensive directory of Accredited AmCham Affiliates throughout the world. An American Chamber of Commerce (AmCham) abroad is a voluntary association of U.S. enterprises and individuals doing business in a given country as well as firms and business executives of that country who operate in the United States.

As members of the U.S. Chamber of Commerce, AmChams benefit from innumerable services and privileges adhering to U.S. Chamber membership.

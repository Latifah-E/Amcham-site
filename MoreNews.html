Dar es Salaam. The US has engaged Tanzania over improving the local business environment demanding the government must now walk the talk. The two sides met on Monday June 17 at the inaugural Tanzania -US business dialogue which sought to discuss challenges facing the companies and concrete actions the government would take to improve the situation.
